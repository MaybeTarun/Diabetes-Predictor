About Diabetes Predictor

Diabetes is one of the most noxious diseases in the world. It can be caused due to obesity or high blood glucose level, and other multitude of reasons. It affects the hormonal insulin, resulting in abnormal metabolism of carbs and increases level of sugar in the blood. Diabetes occurs when body does not make sufficient amount of required insulin. According to (WHO) World Health Organization about 422 million people suffering from diabetes particularly from low- or idle-income countries. And this could be increased to 490 billion up to the year of 2030. Diabetes should not be ignored if it is untreated then Diabetes may cause some major issues in a person like heart related problems, kidney problem, blood pressure, eye damage and it can also affect other organs of human body which can prove fatal to the human body.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Diabetes is one of the most common diseases caused by a group of metabolic disorders. It is also known as Diabetic Mellitus. It affects the various organs of the human body. Now a days, Healthcare industries are generating huge volumes of data. Diabetes is one of the major cause of deaths around the world. If a diabetics patient is left untreated for a long time, it may lead to hazardous problems to the body.

SOLUTION

Early prediction of disease like diabetes can be helpful in controlling them via taking necessary precautions and using proper methods which can help in saving of many human lives lost to such disease which further extend lifespan of disease-inflicted people. To accomplish this, this work explores prediction of diabetes by taking various attributes related to diabetes disease. For this purpose, we use the Pima Indian Diabetes Dataset and Machine Learning.
